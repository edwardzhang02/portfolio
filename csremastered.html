CS Remastered

CS Remastered is a nonprofit that my friends and I started to students to programming at a younger age, focused on providing a 1-on-1 learning experience. I had never even looked into computer science until high school, because I had no clue where to even start to learn - which languages or where to find resources. By reaching out to parents of younger students, we are able to introduce students to programming at a younger age, making the next generation of computer science students more comfortable and experienced with code.
